About

I have been interested in technology and design from a very young age. Growing up, my favorite toys were my Lego bricks and my electronics set that I used to design and then create my inventions. I still have a passion for design, however now I focus on designing and creating applications.

When I create an application, the design and scalability are the two most important factors. Having design as a focus has helped me create applications that focus on user interaction and user experience. My other main focus is scalability. After I complete an algorithm, I examine it for inefficiencies and then redesign and recode until the algorithm is sufficiently scalable. Focusing on design and scalability is very challenging, however, having an end product that is easily usable by many users is well worth the extra time needed to create quality applications.

I write in Java, Python, JavaScript, CSS, and HTML. I also have database experience with Oracle PL/SQL and MySQL. I posted some of my projects on GitHub and also under the projects tab. If you would like to give me any feedback on my projects, please contact me.

I am currently finishing my Masters Degree in Applied Computer Science and working as a teaching assistant. My coursework and teaching has helped me gain a theoretical and practical knowledge of computer science theory. More importantly, I try to apply my knowledge of data structures and algorithms when designing and creating applications.
